Meet Our Team

OLUSEGUN ADENIYI

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Olusegun Adeniyi is a Fellow, Chartered Institute of Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN). He is a fellow member of the Chartered Institute of Bankers in Nigeria (CIBN), Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria (CITN) and Associate member of Nigeria Institute of Management (NIM).

He holds Masters degrees in Banking & Finance (MBF) and Economics (MSc) both from University of Lagos, Nigeria. He has over 22 years work and entrepreneurship experience, having worked with Ecobank (Nig) Plc for 8 years in various capacities as Chief Inspector, Head of Operations, Risk Manager and Relationship Manager and in Stirling Civil Engineering Co Ltd, a foremost construction company in Nigeria as Executive Director, Finance and Administration. Subsequently, he commenced his entrepreneurship journey and has run several businesses successfully till date. He currently sits on their board as Chairman:

- Exact Petroleum Co. Ltd, Dealer and Fleet Manager with NNPC RETAIL LTD.

- DRIVESAFE LTD, a transportation and Haulage company with MRS GROUP.

- SAFETRIP LTD, a passenger transportation company (intercity and intracity transport)

Segun has attended a variety of training programs spanning Property Management, banking operations, treasury, marketing and Retail management. He was the best graduating student in Accounting from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria (1988) and overall best graduating student in Faculty of Administration amongst 600 graduating students.

Overall, Segun is an accomplished businessman with a penchant for discovering opportunities and running businesses profitably.

AYODEJI ADENIYI

Head IT

Ayodeji Adeniyi is a Director in Brightyield Golden Resources Limited. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the prestigious Afe Babalola University, Ekiti state.

He has excellent organization and safety and risk management skills which was garnered during his work experience at Lee Engineering & Construction Company where he worked with the HSE department to ensure safe working conditions with zero incidents or accidents. He also carried out risk assessment exercises.

While in Cam Industries, Kwara State, he worked as an Engineering Trainee in charge of the supervision of the industrial process control for the production of steel wire products in the company. This honed his supervisory and inter-personal skills, all of which he is bringing on board his work at Brightyield Golden Resources Limited.

ADERAYO AJIBULU

Risk & Credit Analyst

Ajibulu Aderayo joined Brightyield Golden Resources Limited from inception of operations and holds the position of the Group Risk Manager. Her role in the company is critical - verification of customers' payslips and bank statements to evaluate and advice on the customer's eligibility for approval of loan request.

She holds a Bachelor's degree from University of Ilorin, Kwara State and has over 5 years work experience in the education and financial sectors. Prior to joining BGRL, she previously worked as Credit Officer at Awesome Credit Limited.

She is focused, result-oriented and possesses good interpersonal communication skills which are valuable to the company.
